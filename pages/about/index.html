Bio

My name is Inês and I’m a Data Scientist. My academic background starts at Faculdade de Engenharia da Universidade do Porto (FEUP) studying Computer Science (Mestrado Integrado em Engenharia Informática e Computação). In the meantime, I’ve studied one semester abroad at Delft University of Technology (Netherlands) on the Data Science and Technology Track.

My relation with industry started early. I’ve done an Internship at Bitmaker Software where I’ve first work within a team and with a client. In my graduation year, I’ve realized my Dissertation as a collaboration between FEUP and DefinedCrowd Corp., developing the thesis Throughput Forecasting for Crowdsourced Text-Enrichment.

Currently, I’m working at DefinedCrowd as a Data Scientist within the R&D department. My work consists of answering to performance and quality problems within the DefinedCrowd’s crowdsourcing platform. As a data scientist, I explore, prepare and analyse the data, create the experimental setup, perform experiments and evaluate the results. I also work closely with the Product and Engineering teams to implement the achieved solution. Finally, I report the project and present the solution attained to the relevant stakeholders.

In my spare time, I like to travel, read, play videogames, play the piano, watch series and movies, cook and try new restaurants (especially vegetarian/plant-based!) and drink Belgian beers. I also enjoy participating in competitions and DSPT Meetups.
